FAQ

Is this safe for my inbox?

Yes. InboxAction connects via official provider APIs and uses read‑only access for your emails. It doesn’t send mail or change anything in your inbox.

Does it work with Outlook or other providers?

Right now we’re starting with Gmail. If you want Outlook, iCloud, or others, tell us in the waitlist form — we’ll prioritize based on demand.

How much will it cost?

During early access, InboxAction will be free while we learn and improve. Later, we expect simple, student‑friendly pricing with a generous free tier.

Who is InboxAction for?

Students, founders, freelancers, and anyone who lives in email and keeps missing tasks, deadlines, or follow‑ups buried in threads.
