Common questions

Is InboxAction safe for my email?

Yes. We use secure, read‑only access through official provider APIs. That means we can read emails to extract tasks, but we can’t send mail, delete anything, or make changes to your inbox.

Which email providers are supported?

We’re starting with Gmail. If you use Outlook, iCloud, or something else, mention it when you join the waitlist — we’ll prioritize what people ask for most.

How much will InboxAction cost?

During early access, InboxAction will be free while we learn and improve the product. Later, we expect simple, affordable pricing with a generous free tier for individuals.

Who is InboxAction for?

Anyone who regularly misses tasks or deadlines because they were buried in email — students, founders, freelancers, and people who live in their inbox all day.
